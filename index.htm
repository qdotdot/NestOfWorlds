The Nest of Worlds

Breed your own music, http://now.hackerspace.pl/

Note: audio is available only on Firefox 4.

About

Nest of Worlds is an interactive environment for designing, playing and sharing musical compositions („worlds”) based upon logic of „Conway's game of Life” - cellular automata simulation of a colony of living cells.

You can set up your own pattern (initial cell colony) on the hex-shaped grid and run them through the life span, listening to their screams and moans, however harmonious they may appear.

You can save parameters of the successful experiments for others to experience, load someone elses work to enjoy or refine, or simply chose random pattern and watch it live till it dies.
